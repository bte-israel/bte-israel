Install an old version needed by our server

1.20

Step 1: Go to the Minecraft Launcher and click the Installations tab at the top.

Step 2: Check if you have version 1.20 installed, I do, but for the sake of the instructions, I don't. If you don't, click the new instalation button.

Step 3: Name your instalation whatever you want, I will call it 1.20, and also search for 1.20 release in the search bar and click release 1.20

Step 4: Click install, if you clicked Launch after Install, it will open after it is finished. If you didn't or you don't know how to re-enter the game, go to the Installations tab at the top, hover over your instalation, and click Play.
